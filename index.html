About

Hyderabad is the captial of telangana. In the hyderabad many famous places are there like Charminar,Hussain sagar,Birlamandir,Imax....etc

The Charminar is a monument in Hyderabad, India. It is one of the tourist attractions in Hyderabad.The fifth ruler of the Qutb Shahi dynasty Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah built the Charminar in 1591.

Hussain Sagar is a necklace shaped lake in Hyderabad built by Ibrahim Quli Qutb Shah, and named after Hussain Shah Wali.Hussain Sagar Lake, situated about 2 km from the heart of the city of Hyderabad

The construction of birla mandir took 10 years and was opened in 1976 by Swami Ranganathananda of Ramakrishna Mission. The temple was constructed by Birla Foundation, which has also constructed several similar temples across India, all of which are known as Birla Mandir.
